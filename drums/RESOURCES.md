# Resources

High-trust sources for building single-stroke speed. Prefer these over
parametric knowledge.

## Primary sources (books / DVDs — the canon)

- **Bill Bachman — _Stick Technique: The Essential Guide for the Modern
  Drummer_** (Hudson Music). The clearest modern treatment of free stroke,
  wrist/finger/Moeller strokes, and how wrist-to-finger ratio changes with
  tempo. Trust: very high.
  - Free sample PDF: https://hudsonmusic.com/wp-content/uploads/Stick-Technique-sample.pdf
- **Jojo Mayer — _Secret Weapons for the Modern Drummer_** (Hudson Music). The
  physics of drumming, fulcrum, rebound, Gladstone/Moeller/finger technique,
  push-pull. Trust: very high.
  - https://hudsonmusic.com/product/secret-weapons-for-the-modern-drummer/
- **Tommy Igoe — _Great Hands for a Lifetime_**. A structured daily hand
  workout; philosophy of relaxation and the fulcrum. Trust: very high.
- **George Lawrence Stone — _Stick Control_** and **_Accents & Rebounds_**. The
  classic pattern books for evening out the hands. Trust: very high (canonical).

## Free / online (good for grounding & quick reference)

- **Bill Bachman — "Top 10 Rudiments Part 1: Single-Stroke Roll"** (Modern
  Drummer). Free article on the free-stroke single-stroke roll.
  - https://www.moderndrummer.com/drum-lessons/bill-bachman-top-10-rudiments-part-1-single-stroke-roll/
- **Bill Bachman — drumworkout.com** ("Extreme Hands Makeover", 450+ videos).
  Step 25 "Moeller Whip to Free Stroke", 16th/32nd build-ups, Purple Singles.
  - https://drumworkout.com/
- **Drumeo — "6 Metronome Hacks to Level Up Your Drumming"**. Burst training,
  tempo trainer, subdivision practice.
  - https://www.drumeo.com/beat/metronome-exercises-for-drummers/
