# Notes & Preferences

## Teaching preferences
- Do NOT praise the user for observations (global rule).
- User is intermediate, disciplined, patient — can handle real drills and
  desirable difficulty. Don't over-simplify.
- Always frame practice around the metronome (user's default).
- Hard budget: **30 min/day**. Every routine must fit.

## Working notes
- Core teaching thesis (grounded in Bachman, Mayer, Igoe): **speed = relaxation +
  free stroke + correct wrist-to-finger ratio**, not muscle. Tension is the enemy.
- Diagnostic-first approach: Lesson 1 doubles as a measurement of the user's
  "clean ceiling" BPM so future lessons have a real baseline.
